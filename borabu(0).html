About Us

Welcome to Borabu Technical Training Institute. We are committed to providing high-quality technical education to our students.

Vision

To be a center of Excellence for Technical, Vocational Education and Training.

Mission

To provide Competent Human Resources for Sustainable Development in Science and Technology.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Professionalism
  • Accountability and Transparency
  • Creativity and Innovativenes
  • Team Work

We offer our learners training and mentorship to ensure that our graduates are all round citizens.
